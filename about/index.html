About

I am currently doing a PhD in Legal Studies and I have an an MA in Philosophy, which was focused on neo-pragmatism and the Philosophy of Language. My current research is focused on Marx, Hegel, Legal Positivism, some Critical Theory (namely, Adorno), and Wilfrid Sellars.

I used to be an academic librarian and, before that, an archivist. Since I am used to planning and revising extensively for writing, this blog is meant to be different, but habits are hard to break. So far, I’ve been sloppy about references and citations, but if you want to know what exactly I’m referencing, I’d be happy to fill you in. You can contact me here.
